PRIVACY POLICY

1. Data Collection

AURA Chronos collects biometric data, including heart rate and SpO2 levels, to provide real-time health insights. All data is encrypted locally on your device.

2. Neural Link Privacy

Our neural engine processes your data on-device. We do not sell your personal health metrics to third-party advertisers.

3. Your Rights

You have the right to export or delete your neural profile at any time through the AURA mobile application settings.
